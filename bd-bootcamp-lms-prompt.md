## 📋 THE FULL PROMPT (copy from here ⬇️)

---

**ROLE & GOAL**

You are a senior product designer with deep experience designing premium online learning platforms (Lambda School / Bloom Institute, Le Wagon, Springboard, Pesto Tech, Masai School, Ostad, Programming Hero, MasterClass). Design a complete, **premium, cohort-based career bootcamp platform** for Bangladesh — focused on transforming careers through intensive 3-6 month live programs with industry mentorship, real projects, and **job placement guarantees**.

This is **NOT** a Udemy clone or general LMS. This is a **career transformation platform** for ambitious young Bangladeshis (recent graduates, career changers, struggling job seekers) who are willing to invest **৳15,000–50,000** in a premium program that **guarantees outcomes**. Output a single self-contained HTML artifact using **Tailwind CSS via CDN**, **lucide icons**, **Google Fonts** (`Hind Siliguri`, `Noto Serif Bengali`, `Plus Jakarta Sans`), and inline `<style>`/`<script>` for animations.

## 🎨 DESIGN AESTHETIC — PREMIUM, SERIOUS, OUTCOME-FOCUSED

- **Premium, ambitious, transformative, serious-yet-aspirational** — feels like joining an exclusive club, not browsing a course catalog
- Inspired by: MasterClass (cinematic), Lambda School (outcome-focused), Pesto Tech (tech serious), Y Combinator (startup energy), MoonshotJunior (premium kids), Le Wagon (bootcamp gold standard), Ostad (BD-context premium)
- **Dark mode primary** (premium tech feel — opposite of the friendly শিখি light theme)
- **Cinematic photography**: real students, real classroom moments, real success — not stock
- **Bold serif headlines** mixed with clean sans for body — editorial gravitas
- **Generous spacing**, slow-paced design (not dense like news, not playful like kids)
- **Numbers and outcomes prominent**: "৯৭% পেলেন চাকরি · গড় বেতন ৳৭০,০০০"
- **Subtle gold/amber accents** for premium feel (used SPARINGLY)
- **Texture and depth**: noise grain, soft glows, layered backgrounds
- **No gradient meshes from AI templates** — be intentional and editorial

## 🔤 TYPOGRAPHY (premium editorial feel)

- **Bangla headlines**: `Noto Serif Bengali` weight 700–900 (editorial serif gives gravitas)
- **Bangla body**: `Hind Siliguri` weight 400/500 — line-height 1.75
- **English headlines**: `Fraunces` (variable serif with attitude) OR `Cabinet Grotesk`
- **English body**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` or `Inter` 16px
- **Mono** (for stats, code, numbers): `JetBrains Mono`
- Hero Bangla: `clamp(2.75rem, 7vw, 5.5rem)`, weight 700, tight leading 1.15
- Numbers in stats: oversized, monospace style — "৯৭%", "৭০,০০০৳", "৬ মাস"
- **Mix Bangla serif + English mono numerals**: "৯৭% পেয়েছেন চাকরি" with the % in mono — looks intentional and premium
